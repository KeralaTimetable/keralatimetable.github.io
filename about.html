About Kerala Timetable

Welcome to Kerala Timetable, your ultimate destination for academic schedules, official notifications, and study resources in Kerala. We know how frustrating it is for students to navigate through spammy websites, fake download buttons, and broken links just to find their exam dates.

That is why we built a centralized, lightning-fast platform focusing primarily on providing accurate timetables, latest updates, and syllabi for KTU B.Tech, Kerala SSLC, Plus One, Plus Two, and other major state examinations.
