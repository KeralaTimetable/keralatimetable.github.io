About Kerala Timetable

Welcome to Kerala Timetable, the fastest-growing independent digital hub dedicated to engineering and high school students across Kerala. We know exactly how frustrating it is to navigate through cluttered, ad-heavy websites just to find a simple exam date or a study module.

Built by students, for students, our platform is designed to be a centralized, lightning-fast resource engine. We specialize in aggregating real-time academic schedules, high-yield study materials, and official university alerts for APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) B.Tech programs, as well as Kerala SSLC and Plus Two state examinations.

What We Offer

Previous Year Questions (PYQs)

Stop wasting time searching for past papers. We host a massive, instantly searchable archive of KTU PYQs. Just type your course code, and download watermarked, high-quality PDFs with zero wait times.

Syllabus-Oriented Notes

Access concise, high-yield study notes crafted specifically for the KTU 2024 Scheme and 2019 Scheme. Our module-wise PDFs help you focus strictly on what matters for your B.Tech semester exams.

Live Exam Timetables

We provide meticulously organized, easy-to-read exam schedules for regular and supplementary exams. Our custom dashboards ensure you never miss a KTU, SSLC, or Plus Two exam date again.

Instant WhatsApp Community

Join thousands of students in our secure, official WhatsApp channel. Get real-time push notifications for university result declarations, timetable shifts, and immediate access to newly uploaded study materials.
