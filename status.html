Frequently Asked Questions

Student portal not opening?

During peak times, especially when B.Tech exam results or timetables are published, the APJAKTU servers experience extreme traffic spikes causing the login page to timeout or show a 504 Gateway error.

Is the result site down?

You can use the live ping tool above to verify if the main KTU website is down globally. If the tool says "Offline," the university servers are down and you will need to wait.
