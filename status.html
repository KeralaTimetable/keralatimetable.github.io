Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the KTU student portal not opening?

During peak times, especially when B.Tech exam results or S2 timetables are published, the APJAKTU servers experience extreme traffic spikes. This causes the KTU student login page to timeout, show a 504 Gateway error, or stop opening entirely.

Is the KTU result site down today?

You can use the live ping tool above to verify if the main KTU website is down globally, or if it's just your internet. If the tool says "Offline" or "Major Outage," the university servers are down and you will need to wait.
