2. Check via App (Fastest)

If the web portals show a "504 Gateway Timeout" on May 15th, the mobile apps are usually hosted on different servers and load much faster.

1

Download the official Saphalam app or PRD Live app from the Google Play Store.

2

Open the app and select "SSLC Results 2026".

3

Enter your Register Number and Date of Birth to view your grades instantly.
