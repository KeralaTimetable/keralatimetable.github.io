KTU Grade System & Mark Distribution

Ever wondered how APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University calculates your final grade? Below is the official UGC-approved grade point table, pass criteria, and the exact formula to convert your CGPA into a percentage.

Marks to Grade Conversion Table

Grade Grade Point % of Total Marks (CIE + ESE)
S 10 90% and above
A+ 9.0 85% and above but less than 90%
A 8.5 80% and above but less than 85%
B+ 8.0 75% and above but less than 80%
B 7.5 70% and above but less than 75%
C+ 7.0 65% and above but less than 70%
C 6.5 60% and above but less than 65%
D 6.0 55% and above but less than 60%
P (Pass) 5.5 50% and above but less than 55%

The Fail (F/FE/I) Conditions

F

Failed

Overall marks (Internal + External) are below 50% OR your End Semester Exam (ESE) marks are below 40%.

FE

Failed Eligibility

Failed due to lack of eligibility criteria (e.g., attendance shortage below the 75%/60% rule).

I

Incomplete

Could not appear for the end semester examination but fulfills all internal eligibility criteria.

KTU CGPA to Percentage Formula

When applying for jobs or higher studies, you will often need to convert your 10-point CGPA into a standard percentage. The official KTU formula is:

Official Conversion Formula

Percentage = 10 × CGPA
